Nicole Phillips

Web Producer/Developer

Monday to Friday, 9-5, I code emails. After those hours and on the weekends, I work on my web development projects. I first got interested in web development and design, in high school. My high school computer teacher had assigned us the task of building a website, using Microsoft FrontPage, I believe. Well, I had the hardest time getting the images and header text, to go where I wanted it to. This led to me, taking it upon myself, to find out how code it by hand. I would further grow my knowledge in college, through updating my Xanga and myspace pages. Also in my core classes for my concentration, Multimedia. It would be in one of these classes, I would be first introduced to Adobe Fireworks & Flash, formerly owned by Macromedia. After working with Fireworks, I finally realized WYSIWYG editors, were not my friends.

I was lucky enough to have more than one professor in my department to encourage my curiosity and my thirst for learning as much as I could about web development. I have over three years experience working in a front end development capacity, ranging from building out microsites & landing pages to the development of HTML emails optimized for viewing in different email clients and mobile devices. Web design & development, is always changing & growing. I look forward to learning what new things they have to offer and to further develop my skills.
